AutoHub Service Drive Acquisition

🎯 What AutoHub Does

AutoHub is an automated vehicle acquisition platform that turns your service drive into a used car sourcing engine. Instead of relying solely on auctions or trade-ins, the system proactively engages customers already in your service bay — people who already trust your dealership — to evaluate and potentially acquire their vehicles.

Within 5–7 minutes of a customer checking in for service, AutoHub sends a text message offering a no-obligation vehicle valuation. The customer can respond right from their phone, and the system handles the rest: automated pricing, appointment scheduling, and handoff to your acquisitions team.

🏎️ What This Means for Audi Baton Rouge

Audi Baton Rouge has no dedicated service-drive acquisition tool currently in place (unlike BHBMW which runs AMP). This makes AutoHub a potentially strong fit — the store is missing a proven source of used inventory that competitors are already capturing.

Used Audi inventory is critical for the store's pre-owned business, and acquiring directly from service customers — who already know and trust Audi Baton Rouge — represents higher-margin opportunities than auction purchases. The lack of any existing automation means AutoHub would be a net-new revenue channel rather than a replacement.
