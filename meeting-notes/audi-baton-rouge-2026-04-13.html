🧠 Strategy Notes

Differentiation is the challenge. All Audi websites look the same right now. We must differentiate on message (rate buy-downs) and humanity (staff videos). If the car and the site are the same, the customer just shops on price.


The "Reserved" trick. Keeping sold inventory on the site as "Reserved" instead of removing it immediately helps maintain search volume and shows the market is moving, even if the specific car isn't available.


Fixed Ops is the engine. With service gross up $15k and parts up $5k, the store is healthy despite a slow sales month. Focus on capturing every possible warranty hour to close the gap on lagging stores.


Vendor Redundancy. Flick Fusion for video follow-up is likely redundant if we actually use the tools already built into Podium. Train staff on the current stack before adding more software overhead.
