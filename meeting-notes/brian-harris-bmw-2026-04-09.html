🧠 Strategy Notes

The market is a want vs. need economy right now. Traffic is strong — people are shopping — but they're watching their money closely. Don't throw more ad budget at the problem. Be present, stay top of mind, and make it easy when they're ready to pull the trigger.


Sedans are back because of gas prices. The 3 Series is legitimately competitive on fuel economy with small imports. Lead with that angle in SEO pages and social content — it's a real differentiator right now.


EVs are a quiet opportunity. 17 people shopped EVs and didn't buy in the last 90 days. With Tesla opening the Supercharger network to BMW, the charging objection is gone. Gas prices make the case for you. A targeted one-to-one email to those 17 people costs almost nothing.


Expired ads are leaking credibility. Wrong prices in paid search send people to a site that says something different. Fix immediately — this erodes trust and wastes ad spend every day it's live.


January momentum trick works. Carrying December incentives into January beat the traditional post-holiday slump. People who were thinking about it in December converted in January because the deal was still there. Remember this for Q4 2026.
